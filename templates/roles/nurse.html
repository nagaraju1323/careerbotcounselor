Nursing is a dynamic and rewarding profession that plays a vital role in patient care and public health. Nurses work in hospitals, clinics, schools, and communities, offering both medical and emotional support to patients.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitoring patient health and administering medications
  • Assisting in surgeries and emergency care
  • Educating patients and families about health conditions
  • Collaborating with doctors and healthcare teams

Eligibility Criteria

  • Diploma, GNM, B.Sc Nursing, or higher degrees (e.g., M.Sc Nursing)
  • Registration with State Nursing Council
  • Compassion, communication skills, and clinical knowledge
