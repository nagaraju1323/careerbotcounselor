Surgeons are highly trained medical professionals who perform operations to treat injuries, diseases, and deformities. This career demands precision, stamina, and years of rigorous education and hands-on training.

Key Responsibilities

  • Performing surgeries and post-operative care
  • Diagnosing conditions requiring surgical intervention
  • Collaborating with medical teams and specialists
  • Maintaining sterile environments and surgical protocols

Eligibility Criteria

  • Complete 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Qualify NEET and complete MBBS (5.5 years)
  • Pursue MS/MD in a surgical specialty (3 years)
  • Optional: Super-specialization via MCh or DNB
