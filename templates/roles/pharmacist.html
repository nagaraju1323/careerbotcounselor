Pharmacists are essential healthcare professionals who ensure the safe and effective use of medications. They work in hospitals, retail pharmacies, research labs, and pharmaceutical companies, playing a critical role in patient care and drug development.

Key Responsibilities

  • Dispensing prescription medications accurately
  • Advising patients on dosage, side effects, and interactions
  • Collaborating with doctors and healthcare teams
  • Monitoring patient health and medication effectiveness

Eligibility Criteria

  • Complete 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Math
  • Pursue D.Pharm (2 years) or B.Pharm (4 years)
  • Optional: M.Pharm or Pharm.D for advanced roles
  • Register with State Pharmacy Council
