Full Stack Developers design and build both the front-end and back-end of web applications. They bring ideas to life by weaving together user interfaces, servers, APIs, and databases to create seamless digital experiences.

Key Responsibilities

  • Building responsive user interfaces (HTML/CSS/JS/React)
  • Designing scalable backend logic (Node.js, Python, Java)
  • Creating and managing RESTful APIs and databases
  • Deploying and maintaining full web stacks on the cloud

Eligibility Criteria

  • Any graduate degree (preferably in CS or IT)
  • Proficiency in at least one frontend and backend stack
  • Project experience or portfolio showcasing practical skills
  • Optional: Coding bootcamps or certifications in MERN/LAMP stack
