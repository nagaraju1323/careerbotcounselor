Becoming a doctor is one of the most respected and impactful professions. Doctors diagnose, treat, and care for patients across various specialties, contributing to public health and medical advancement.

Key Responsibilities

  • Diagnosing illnesses and prescribing treatments
  • Performing surgeries and medical procedures
  • Monitoring patient recovery and progress
  • Collaborating with healthcare teams and specialists

Eligibility Criteria

  • Complete 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Qualify NEET and pursue MBBS (5.5 years including internship)
  • Optional: Specialize through MD/MS or super-specialty programs
