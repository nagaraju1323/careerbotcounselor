Data Scientists are analytics experts who use statistical methods, algorithms, and machine learning to uncover insights from massive datasets. They blend technical skills with curiosity to solve real-world problems across industries.

Key Responsibilities

  • Analyzing data using Python, R, SQL, and tools like pandas or scikit-learn
  • Building predictive models and machine learning pipelines
  • Communicating findings with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, matplotlib)
  • Collaborating with cross-functional teams for data-driven decisions

Eligibility Criteria

  • Undergraduate degree in STEM (CS, Statistics, Math, Engineering)
  • Proficiency in Python, statistics, data analysis, and visualization
  • Optional: Certifications (IBM Data Science, Google Data Analytics, etc.)
  • Strong foundation in linear algebra, probability, and ML concepts
